Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Find Mouse.

Find Mouse is a hidden object puzzle mobile game where you scan beautifully illustrated scenes to spot a small mouse hidden in each level. It features 100+ progressively challenging scenes and is free to play on iOS and Android.
Yes — Find Mouse is completely free to download and play on both iOS (Apple App Store) and Android (Google Play). No subscription, no hidden fees.
Find Mouse is available on iOS devices (iPhone and iPad) via the Apple App Store, and on Android phones and tablets via Google Play.
Absolutely. Find Mouse is designed for all ages. Its relaxing, non-violent hidden object gameplay is enjoyable for children, adults, and seniors alike.
Find Mouse features over 100 uniquely illustrated scenes, with new levels added regularly. Difficulty increases as you progress — from easy cozy rooms to complex, busy scenes.
Find Mouse was created by Raiz Games, an indie mobile game studio passionate about crafting relaxing, beautifully illustrated puzzle games for everyone.
