About the Game

Find Mouse is a relaxing hidden object puzzle game where players scan beautifully illustrated scenes to find a cleverly hidden mouse. Each level is hand-crafted to be visually rich and satisfying to search, making it the perfect game to unwind with.

The gameplay is simple: look carefully at each illustrated scene and tap the mouse the moment you spot it. Scenes grow progressively more detailed and challenging as you advance — going from cozy kitchens to bustling markets to dense forest floors — keeping the game fresh for hundreds of levels.

Find Mouse is designed for all ages. Whether you're a child sharpening attention, an adult looking to de-stress, or a grandparent enjoying a relaxing pastime, Find Mouse fits every lifestyle. No timers, no pressure — just the joy of finding the mouse.

About Raiz Games

Raiz Games is an independent mobile game studio focused on building accessible, high-quality puzzle games that anyone can enjoy. We believe great games don't need to be complicated — they need to be thoughtful, beautiful, and fun.

Find Mouse is our first published title, and we pour care and creativity into every illustrated scene. Our goal is to keep expanding the game with new scenes, seasonal updates, and fresh mechanics based on player feedback.

We're a small team with a big love for craft. Every level in Find Mouse is carefully composed to balance challenge and satisfaction — we want that "aha!" moment when you finally spot the mouse to feel genuinely rewarding.

Brain Benefits

Hidden object games like Find Mouse aren't just entertainment — they offer real cognitive benefits. Research on visual search tasks shows that regularly searching complex scenes improves:

Observation skills — You train your eye to detect subtle differences and notice small details that most people overlook. After 10 levels of Find Mouse, players typically report seeing the world more attentively.

Focus and sustained attention — Each scene requires you to maintain concentration as you systematically scan the image. This kind of deliberate focus is a form of mindfulness that reduces stress.

Visual memory — Remembering which areas you've already searched, and recalling visual patterns from earlier levels, exercises short-term visual memory in a natural, enjoyable way.
