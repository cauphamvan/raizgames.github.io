Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

✦ Overview

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how Find Mouse ("the App") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our application.

1 Information We Collect

Personal Information:

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number — unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., when contacting support).

Non-Personal Information:

The App may automatically gather the following in anonymous or aggregated formats:

  • Device specifications and operating system details
  • Gameplay metrics and usage statistics
  • Diagnostic logs and crash reports
  • Approximate location based on IP address

Advertising & Tracking Data:

Third-party advertising partners may collect the following to deliver relevant ads:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA / GAID)
  • Device identifiers
  • Ad interaction data

2 How We Use Information

Collected data is used to:

  • Enhance gameplay experience and performance
  • Analyze app usage and fix technical issues
  • Display advertisements
  • Manage in-app purchases and subscriptions

3 Third-Party Services

The App integrates third-party services that process data according to their own privacy policies:

🔥 Firebase Analytics 🛡️ Firebase Crashlytics 📢 Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics — tracks user behavior to improve the app
  • Firebase Crashlytics — identifies and helps fix crashes
  • Google AdMob — displays advertisements within the App

4 In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Payments are processed securely through Apple Inc. and Google. We do not store or have access to your payment information such as credit card details.

5 Advertising and Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS, you will receive a tracking permission request in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

  • Allowing tracking — enables personalized, more relevant ads
  • Declining — ads will still be displayed with reduced personalization

6 Children's Privacy

Find Mouse is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under the age of 13. If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

7 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Analytics providers
  • Advertising partners
  • Payment processors

All sharing is limited to what is necessary to provide the App's services.

8 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9 Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

10 Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to deliver our services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.
